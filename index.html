Quick Bio

Meet Mr. Meyerowitz

Mr. Meyerowitz has been an educator for twenty years in Phoenix, AZ, and in San Jose, CA. In that time, he has taught about twenty unique Humanities courses--APs, electives, post-APs, and Capstones--and enjoys creating classes that match his students' ever-changing interests. He also loves animals (he currently has five pets plus two foster kittens), The Simpsons, and the histories of post-colonial lands and peoples. In his free time (is time ever free?), you'll find him with a book, on the beach, or scheming to make the Humanities Great Again.
